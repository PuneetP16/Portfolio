How to Kill Procrastination?

Now that it is understood what is Procrastination, lets see how can we kill it.

Below are the steps to remove procrastination for good.

1. Understanding the hard truth

  • Nobody cares who you are!
  • STOP Blaming or Being A Victim (Like I was)
  • The world only responds to your action or what you produce
  • You Are What You DO!

2. Realize Your Value

  • The ODDS of being A Human being are 400 Trillion to 1.
  • There are 7 Billion Human Beings - You are already at the top because of all you have!
  • You are a fantastic, precious creation of nature
  • YOUR Life is Legendary?
  • YOU deserve to Live Your Dream Life - Don't waste a second!
  • Think what do Mukesh Ambani, Bill Gates, and You have in common?

3. Find Your WHY

  • Find the reason You want to LIVE YOUR DREAM LIFE.
  • To give your loved ones the time, the gifts, the environment, the pampering they deserve?
  • To give back to this fantastic world in any form?
  • To be remembered as a Legend in your field?
  • WHAT IS YOUR LIFE'S MISSION?
  • You should write: "I want to Live My Dream Life Because…….."

4. Remove the 2 Hurdles

  1. Instant Gratification Activities (Dopamine Detox)
  2. Your 1 Toxic Habit (Use I.E.R)

1st Hurdle: Instant Gratification Activities (Dopamine Detox)

  • Block ALL Notifications in your phone.
  • Go to Settings -> Notifications
  • Turn notifications off for ALL Apps
  • This includes YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, & ALL News apps etc.
  • Such a Simple Step can itself show immediate benefits.
  • Digital Detox - For the next 24 Hours - No Videos of any Kind, No Social Media, No Video Games, no songs.
  • You can only read, write, talk, walk, eat simple food.

2nd Hurdle: Your 1 Toxic Habit (Use I.E.R)

  • I for identify: What is the one unproductive habit that takes up all your spare time?
  • E for Eliminate: Remove it from the root. Never do it again.
  • R for Replace: With 1 productive habit which contributes to your long-term growth. Make it easily accessible. Productive habits can be anything reading, writing, training or being with family.
  • 1 toxic habit wastes 80-90% of your time. Implementing this TODAY will dramatically transform you day.

5. Take Action NOW.

  1. STEP 1: Write Down " I need to Live My Dream Life because..."
  2. STEP 2: Block ALL app notifications & go on a Digital Detox for 24 hours.
  3. STEP 3: Identify, Eliminate and Replace your one TOXIC HABIT.
